Fear: Irrational
Anqi Meng
This project focuses on three kinds of specific phobias, astrophobia - fear of outer space, hippopotomonstrosesquippedaliophobia- fear of long words and catoptrophobia - fear of mirror. Each of the posters aims to address one phobia. I try to reveal the patientsâ€™ reaction towards phobias by making different effects on the text, such as making the phobia triggers smaller, reflecting the text and hiding certain words. The posters are double-sided: information side shows my research and graphic side visually communicates the phobias. These posters also comes with three motion graphics matching the graphic side, which graphically conveys the feelings of three different phobias.
